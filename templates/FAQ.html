Crackfic is a website designed by a small team of UCI students aiming to replicate the popular word game Madlibs. What makes Crackfic unique is the ability to write stories that have a specific theme/media, instead of the typical randomized stories that the traditional Madlibs is known for. A user can pick their favorite book/movie/cartoon/etc and add their own words to change a randomly chosen template/story that is specific towards their interest.

Like the well-known game of Madlibs, Crackfic takes a story that’s already been pre-written and removes random words that can be replaced with whatever the user desires. Crackfic specifies the parts of speech that it will be replacing, also giving examples to aid the user in changing the words. When completed, the words will be bolded in the finished story, allowing users to see their impact and hopefully have a good laugh.

You can search through our available fandoms by narrowing your search with categories and then the fandom itself (categorized by alphabetical order).

The title of Crackfic derives from more the fandom slang for the word “Crack,” which describes fanworks with ridiculous, unbelievable, incredible, or just silly elements. Since the changed stories are likely to become dumb or inheritable, the term Crackfic felt fitting for what is produced.
